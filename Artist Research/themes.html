Themes, Methods and Techniques

  • Art:
    She is a swedish visual artist and a photo artist. She has worked a lot with staged photos.

  • Themes:
    She made most of her paintings related to nature and one of the themes she often used was “Back to the Nature” and she likes criminology and therefore that is one of the themes she uses.

  • Techniques and Mediums:
    Mediums she used were often photography and sculptures but in the early 1990's she started to use acrylic glass. Techniques she uses include associative imagery.
